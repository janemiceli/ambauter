An addiction to smoking is like slowly falling over a cliff; I would suggest screaming for help if you can't climb up yourself.

-Angela Bauter

Smoking does make one thing: Orphans. I have a friend that can attest to this.
From my friends Live Journal:

So, for those of you who don't know, my dad died a few years back. He has other complications, but mainly it was lung cancer.

So now my mom is in a hospice and I am having to make funeral arrangements and all that crap. And its weird. And surreal.

Know why she's dying? Lung cancer.

Smoking sucks.

I find it amazing that people in pain aren't alowed to have medicinal marijuana but cigarettes are perfectly acceptable even though it kills.

That's what caused this cancer. There is no history of lung cancer, or cancer for that matter, in my family's history. The doctors in both cases said it was almost surely the cigarettes.

So, Friends, PLEASE never smoke and encourage your loved ones to stop. every time my uncle lights up I feel like I could cry. I know most of the people who read this don't smoke cigarettes, but if you're ever tempted... don't. Just don't.
