Let them know when, and make sure they remember

      Nowadays, people are sending out save the date cards or magnets before they send invitations. Usually for weddings, but not always. Mainly, this just gives them even more time to plan, and allows for a little more informal notice. For more on the ideas behind 'Save the Date' follow one of the links on the left. On a sidenote; Magnets would also make neat favors at the reception.

      Save the Date' cards are a simple and compact way to get your message accross; But, why a magnet, instead of a card? The main reason, as I see it, is that everytime they get hungry, they will be reminded of when your wedding is. Cards can be lost, and forgotten, unlike a magnet. They will know exactly where to look for it when they need it.

      Sizes for cards and magnets are 3.5 x 2 inches, and there are also 5.4 x 4.2 inch cards available right now from Vistaprint, fairly cheaply, that come with envelopes.
